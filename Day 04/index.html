Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tuscan Butter
  • Florentine Flour

Method

  1. Crack eggs, beat into sugar
  2. Beat butter, add to egg/sugar mixture
  3. Pour in bowl, place in oven

Snickerdoodle

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Turbinado Sugar

Method

  1. Add sugar and flour together
  2. Cream the butter
  3. Stir together. Place in oven.
